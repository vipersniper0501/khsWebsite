Where to Be and When

KHS uses a block-type schedule. There are 'Red Days' and 'White Days'. Everyday, students will go to 4 periods. Red Days will have students go to their first 4 periods, while White Days will have students go to periods 5, 6, 7, as well as advisory. This means that classes are longer than a typical 7 period schedule, but at least you don't have to go to all of your classes every day. On Thursdays, students get to sleep in an hour; but don't worry, buses are also an hour late. KHS also has a unique lunch plan. Depending on the student's 3rd period teacher and advisory teacher, students will be placed in either A, B, or C lunch. We apologise for the confusion, but we hope this page helps!
