Interested in Joining a Club?

Kaiserslautern High has many clubs to offer, whether you're into computers, art, or just want to find some good friends. Many clubs already exist, so it shouldn't be difficult to fit in somewhere. Explore some of the options!

Interested in Computers? Who do you think made this website? Corvyn Register did.

From clubs like Cyberpatriots to Robotics and National Technical Honors Society, there's plenty to code. Talk to Mr. Robinson or Mr. Smith if interested in any of these clubs!

What about music? Mr. Helston's got you covered.

You like jazz? Or maybe you're more interested in Concert Band? There's also K-Pop and Tri-M Honors Music Society. Music clubs are a great way to meet new people with similar interests!

How about Government Clubs? Are you a leader?

There are plenty of events to plan and policies to be changed. With Student Council, Model United Nations, Model US Senate, and the Prom Committee, there are plenty of things to do! If interested, see Mr. Fairchild or Ms. Standiford

Not interested in any of these?

Don't worry, Kaiserslautern High School has plenty of other clubs. If there's a club you're looking for it's alphabetized. Not finding a club? Make one!
